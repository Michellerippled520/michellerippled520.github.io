## 🕹️ Controls and Gameplay

Use your keyboard to move the snake:

*   Up Arrow: Moves the snake upward.
*   Down Arrow: Moves the snake downward.
*   Left Arrow: Moves the snake to the left.
*   Right Arrow: Moves the snake to the right.

The goal stays simple. Collect the items that appear on the screen to increase your length and your total score. The game ends if you hit the walls or if the snake runs into its own body. 

## 💾 Saving Your Progress

The game keeps track of your best results automatically. It uses your browser's local memory to store your high score. Even if you close your browser or turn off your computer, your best score remains ready for your next session. Please note that clearing your browser cache or deleting your browser history might reset the recorded high score.
